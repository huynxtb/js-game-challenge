AstroFarm: Colony Survival

Introduction:
-----------------
AstroFarm: Colony Survival is a text-based, turn-driven resource management game set on a distant planet. You are in charge of managing your colony's futuristic farm, balancing water, energy, and nutrients to grow various crops. Your goal is to maintain colony health and morale while ensuring sufficient food supply to survive 30 days and reach sustainability.

Gameplay:
-----------------
- Each turn represents one day on the colony.
- You manage three main resources: water, energy, and nutrients.
- You can plant, water, harvest crops, or wait each day.
- Crops have distinct growth times and resource consumption.
- Random environmental events will affect your resources or crops, adding challenges.
- Maintain colony health and morale by keeping food and resources adequately supplied.

Win Condition:
- Survive a full 30 days with no critical shortages in resources or colony health.

Loss Condition:
- Any resource (water, energy, nutrients) reaches zero.
- Colony health falls to zero.

Setup Instructions:
-----------------
1. Ensure you have Node.js installed on your machine.
2. Save the game code into a file named astrofarm_colony_survival.js
3. Open a terminal or command prompt.
4. Navigate to the directory where you saved the file.
5. Run the game with the command: node astrofarm_colony_survival.js

How to Play:
-----------------
- You will be shown colony status and resource levels each day.
- Enter numbers to choose actions:
  1: Plant crops - choose from Martian Potatoes, Solar Wheat, or Alien Berries.
  2: Water crops - speeds up crop growth but consumes additional water.
  3: Harvest crops - collect food from mature crops.
  4: Wait - take no action and move to the next day.
- Random events can positively or negatively impact your colony.
- Monitor your resources carefully and keep colonists fed to maintain morale and health.

Enjoy managing your AstroFarm colony on the new world and see if you can survive the 30-day challenge!
