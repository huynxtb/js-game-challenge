Chrono Colony - Text-based Colony Management Game\n\nGame Introduction:\nChrono Colony is a console-based text game where you build and manage a small colony on a distant planet. You must wisely allocate resources such as water, food, shelter materials, and energy while surviving alien weather patterns, equipment failures, and encounters with strange aliens over 30 in-game days. Each day you select a strategic action to gather resources, reinforce shelters, explore, or rest while managing risks and resources. Your goal is to keep your colony members alive and thriving for 30 days.\n\nSetup Instructions:\n- Ensure you have Node.js installed on your system (https://nodejs.org).\n- Download the game file 'chrono_colony.js' to your computer.\n- Open a terminal or command prompt.\n\nHow to Run the Game:\n- Navigate to the directory containing 'chrono_colony.js'.\n- Run the game by executing: node chrono_colony.js\n- Follow the on-screen prompts to play the game.\n- Each turn represents one day; input the number corresponding to the action you want to perform each day.\n- Survive until day 30 to win!\n- Press Ctrl+C anytime to quit the game.\n\nGame Controls:\n- When prompted, enter the number matching the daily action you want to take. Actions include gathering resources, reinforcing shelters, exploring the planet, or resting.\n- The game will display current colony status and events each turn to inform your decisions.\n\nEnjoy managing your colony in the harsh environment of Chrono Colony!
