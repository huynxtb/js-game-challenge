ChronoHarbor - Console-based Harbor Management Game

Introduction:
ChronoHarbor is a unique text-based management game where you take the role of the harbor master of a mystical time-crossing port. Ships from various timelines and worlds dock at your harbor bringing valuable goods and challenges. Your mission is to manage ship docking schedules, inventory of rare resources, and harbor facility health over a 30-day season. Success is measured by your ability to build wealth while maintaining your facilities and upgrading docks.

Setup Instructions:
- Ensure you have Node.js installed on your system.
- Save the provided JavaScript code into a file named 'chronoharbor.js'.

How to Run the Game:
- Open your terminal or command prompt.
- Navigate to the folder containing 'chronoharbor.js'.
- Run the game with the command: node chronoharbor.js

Gameplay Overview:
- Each turn represents one day at the harbor.
- At the start of each day, new ships will arrive with different cargo.
- You will assign docking slots to arriving ships to unload their cargo.
- Unloaded cargo adds to your resource inventory and profits.
- Random events may affect your facilities or offer trade opportunities.
- Use wealth to repair your harbor facilities or upgrade docks to increase throughput.
- You may consume resources to boost facility health.
- The goal is to reach 10,000 gold coins and manage for 30 days without the harbor falling into disrepair.

Controls:
- Answer prompts in the console (usually 'y' or 'n' or numbers).
- Follow on-screen instructions carefully to schedule ships and manage resources.

Win Condition:
- Accumulate at least 10,000 gold coins by day 30.

Loss Condition:
- Facility health drops to zero.
- Running out of essential resources and low wealth preventing operations.

Enjoy managing your ChronoHarbor and may your timeline prosper!
