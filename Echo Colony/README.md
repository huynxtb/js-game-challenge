Echo Colony - Console Edition

Introduction:
Echo Colony is a turn-based resource management and exploration game set on a mysterious alien planet. You take on the role of a colony manager, tasked with balancing the daily needs of your colonists while exploring the alien landscape, uncovering new resources, facing threats, and striving to build a thriving, sustainable colony.

Setup & Running Instructions:
- Ensure you have Node.js installed on your system.
- Save the game code into a file named 'echo_colony.js'.
- Open your terminal or command prompt.
- Navigate to the directory where 'echo_colony.js' is saved.
- Run the game by typing: node echo_colony.js

How to Play:
- Each turn represents a day in your colony's life.
- You'll see your colony's current status and be prompted for a command.
- Commands:
  * explore  - Send out an expedition to uncover new resources, threats, or artifacts.
  * status   - Display the current state of your colony's population and resources.
  * wait     - Skip exploration and focus on managing resources for the day.
  * help     - Show instructions again.
  * exit     - Quit the game.

Objectives:
- Win the game by growing your colony population to at least 50 colonists and maintaining a stable surplus of food, energy, and materials for 10 consecutive days.
- Avoid loss by keeping your population alive, ensuring food and energy never run out, and protecting your colony from major calamities.

Game Mechanics:
- Your colonists consume food and energy daily.
- Exploration can provide helpful resources or pose threats that reduce your population or resources.
- Random events each day can positively or negatively impact the colony.
- Population growth depends on resource availability.

Enjoy managing your Echo Colony and may your settlers thrive on this alien world!
