Galactic Outpost - Console Text-Based Game

Introduction:
Galactic Outpost puts you in command of a remote interstellar outpost in deep space.
Your objective is to survive 30 days (turns) while managing resources such as energy, supplies, and crew morale.
You must allocate energy each day towards defense, exploration, or repairs, fend off space hazards, and keep your crew motivated.
Win by surviving all 30 days or lose if your outpost is destroyed or morale falls to zero.

Setup Instructions:
1. Ensure you have Node.js installed on your computer (https://nodejs.org/).
2. Save the provided code into a file named galactic_outpost.js.

How to Run:
Open your command line interface (Terminal or Command Prompt).
Navigate to the directory where galactic_outpost.js is saved.
Run the game by typing:

    node galactic_outpost.js

Gameplay:
- Each day, review your outpost's status.
- Decide how to allocate your limited energy resources.
- Random space events can affect your outpost positively or negatively.
- Respond to attacks using turn-based combat when needed.
- Manage your supplies consumption and crew morale carefully.
- Survive until reinforcements arrive on day 31 to win.

Controls:
- Input your decisions by typing the option number when prompted and press Enter.
- Invalid inputs will prompt you to retry with graceful messaging.

Enjoy commanding your Galactic Outpost and good luck!
