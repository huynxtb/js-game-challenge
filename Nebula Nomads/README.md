Nebula Nomads

Introduction:
In Nebula Nomads, you command a spaceship crew navigating a hostile, resource-scarce nebula region. Your goal is to manage your ship's fuel, food, oxygen, and integrity carefully while overcoming random events to successfully reach the home sector within 20 days, keeping at least one crew member alive.

Setup Instructions:
Ensure you have Node.js installed on your system. The game requires no external dependencies.

How to Run:
1. Save the provided code into a file named nebula_nomads.js.
2. Open your terminal/command prompt.
3. Navigate to the directory containing nebula_nomads.js.
4. Run the game with the command:

   node nebula_nomads.js

Gameplay Overview:
- The game runs in turns, each representing one day.
- Each day you will see your current status: resources, crew, ship integrity, and upgrades.
- Random events each turn will challenge your decision-making.
- Manage resources wisely: fuel to move and evade, food and oxygen for your crew's survival, materials for ship upgrades and repairs.
- Upgrade your ship's systems with materials you find to improve survival odds.
- You will need to make critical decisions during alien encounters and equipment failures.
- The objective is to survive 20 days and reach the home sector with at least one crew member alive.

Enjoy your journey and may the stars guide you home!
