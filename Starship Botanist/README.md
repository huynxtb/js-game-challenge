Starship Botanist - Text-Based Console Game\n\nIntroduction:\nYou are a botanist stranded on a crashed starship on an alien planet. Your mission is to cultivate and manage alien plants to produce oxygen, ensuring survival while accumulating resources to repair the ship's engines and escape. The game is turn-based, each turn representing one day where you manage resources, cultivate plants, explore the environment, and handle random events. Maintain oxygen levels and resource management is critical to success.\n\nSetup Instructions:\n- You only need Node.js installed on your computer (version 12 or higher recommended).\n- Save the provided code to a file named starship_botanist.js.\n\nHow to Run:\n- Open a terminal or command prompt.\n- Navigate to the directory containing starship_botanist.js.\n- Run the game by typing: node starship_botanist.js\n- Follow the on-screen instructions and prompts to play.\n\nGame Overview:\n- Each day you choose one action: plant new alien species, explore for resources, or idle to conserve resources.\n- Managing oxygen, water, and energy is vital. Plants consume water and energy while producing oxygen after maturation.\n- Exploring yields chance events like new seeds or resource boosts but consumes water and energy.\n- Random events can challenge your resource balance or plant health.\n- Repair engines by maintaining good resource levels and growing plants. Win by completing repairs and surviving for 30 consecutive days.\n- Lose if oxygen hits zero, critical resources deplete, or all plants die before repairs are done.\n\nEnjoy the strategic challenge of survival and cultivation in Starship Botanist!
