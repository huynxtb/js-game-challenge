Starship Salvage
================

Introduction:
Starship Salvage is a text-based console game where you play as the captain of a salvage crew exploring derelict starships stranded in deep space. Your mission is to collect valuable salvage resources while surviving random hazards and managing your crew's stamina and your ship's integrity.

Objective:
Collect at least 100 units of salvage resources and then rest to safely return to your base. Beware of hazards such as alien encounters and equipment malfunctions that can damage your ship or exhaust your crew.

Gameplay:
- On each turn, you will see the status of your ship integrity, crew stamina, and salvage collected.
- You can choose to:
  - Explore: Spend stamina to find salvage but risk hazards.
  - Salvage: Use stamina to collect salvage with some risk.
  - Repair: Spend salvage to repair your ship's integrity.
  - Rest: Skip exploration to restore crew stamina; if you have enough salvage, resting will end the game successfully.

Win Condition:
Collect at least 100 salvage units and then rest to end the mission successfully.

Loss Conditions:
- Ship integrity reaches zero (ship destroyed).
- Crew stamina reaches zero (crew incapacitated).

Setup & Running:
1. Make sure you have Node.js installed on your system.
2. Save the provided code to a file named "starship_salvage.js".
3. Open a terminal and navigate to the folder containing the file.
4. Run the game using the command:

   node starship_salvage.js

5. Follow on-screen prompts to play the game.

Have fun commanding your salvage crew and navigate the dangers of deep space salvage missions!
