Stellar Salvage: The Asteroid Haul - Console Game

Introduction:
-----------------
"Stellar Salvage: The Asteroid Haul" is a text-based resource management and strategy game played entirely in the console using Node.js. You pilot a salvage spaceship navigating through an asteroid belt, collecting rare minerals while managing limited fuel, cargo space, and ship integrity. Strategic choices, turn-based decisions, and randomized space events will challenge your ability to survive and succeed.

Objective:
-----------------
- Collect and safely return at least 100 units of rare minerals to your base.
- Avoid running out of fuel or sustaining critical damage to your ship.

Core Mechanics:
-----------------
- Each turn, choose actions such as moving forward, mining asteroids, repairing, refueling, or scanning.
- Resources to manage include fuel, cargo capacity, and ship integrity.
- Experience random events like discovering valuable asteroids, encountering hazards, and finding repair/fuel stations.
- Return to base to offload minerals and restock.

How to Play:
-----------------
1. Ensure you have Node.js installed on your system.
2. Save the game code into a file named 'stellar_salvage_the_asteroid_haul.js'.
3. Open a terminal or command prompt.
4. Run the game by typing:

   node stellar_salvage_the_asteroid_haul.js

5. Follow on-screen prompts and input choices by typing numbers and pressing Enter.

Game Controls Summary:
-----------------
At Base:
- 1) Launch into asteroid belt
- 2) Offload minerals
- 3) Repair ship (uses fuel)
- 4) Refuel ship
- 5) Quit game

In Asteroid Belt:
- 1) Move forward (consume fuel, chance to find events)
- 2) Scan area (consume fuel to detect asteroids nearby)
- 3) Mine asteroid (if present)
- 4) Return to base
- 5) Quit game

Winning the Game:
-----------------
Collect and offload at least 100 units of rare minerals at your base station.

Losing the Game:
-----------------
- Running out of fuel while away from base.
- Ship integrity falling to zero from damage.
- Choosing to quit the game.

Enjoy your adventure in the stars, Captain!
